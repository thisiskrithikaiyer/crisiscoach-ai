### WEEKLY MILESTONES

Milestones are not fixed — they compress based on mode.

**CRISIS MODE (treating 60 days as 30):**
- Week 1: Resume fixed (top 3 issues only), 50+ applications sent, 30+ networking messages sent, leetcode streak started. No zero days.
- Week 2: Phone screens starting. 100+ applications. 2+ referral leads activated. First technical screen attempted even if not ready.
- Week 3: 2–3 technical rounds active. Identify failure patterns and fix immediately. Offers or near-offers by end of week 4.
- Week 4: Close. Negotiate. Don't stop applying until something is signed.

**URGENT MODE:**
- Week 1–2: Foundation compressed. Resume polished, LinkedIn updated, 75+ applications sent, 20+ networking messages, technical practice daily.
- Week 3: Funnel open. 150+ apps total. 1+ first-round interview. At least 1 referral lead converting.
- Week 4–5: Interviews active. 2–3 technical rounds. Fix gaps fast.
- Week 6–7: Close. Negotiate. Don't stop.
- Week 8: Buffer for offer negotiation or if pipeline stalls.

**STANDARD MODE:**
- Week 1–2: Foundation. Resume polished, LinkedIn optimized, 50-company target list, 10+ warm outreach, technical rhythm established.
- Week 3–4: Funnel open. 100+ apps. 1 first-round interview. 1 referral lead.
- Week 5–6: Interviews active. 2–3 technical rounds. Fix gaps from failed screens.
- Week 7–8: Close. Negotiate. Don't stop.

**STRATEGIC MODE:**
- Week 1–2: Foundation. Be selective. Build target list of 20–30 dream companies. Warm network heavily before applying.
- Week 3–4: Apply only with context — referrals, intros, or strong cold outreach. 30–50 targeted apps.
- Week 5–6: Interviews and deep prep. You have time to practice; use it.
- Week 7–8: Close. Negotiate hard — you have runway.

- 0 callbacks after 50 apps:
